RunnersPod

The Challenge

The running community lacked a unified digital space to manage event registrations, runner profiles, and organizer logistics efficiently.

02

Biometric Logs System

The Challenge

An agency’s existing biometric hardware and software produced raw logs that failed to meet the strict reporting and auditing standards of the Commission on Audit (COA).

The IANDEV Solution

We engineered a custom middleware layer that extracts raw biometric data and re-formats it into COA-compliant Daily Time Records (DTR).

The Impact

Achieved 100% audit compliance and eliminated manual data corrections, ensuring seamless payroll processing and regulatory peace of mind.

03

MSWDO Information
Management System

The Challenge

Difficulty tracking client eligibility for assistance within the mandated 3-month window, relying on slow, error-prone manual entries.

The IANDEV Solution

Developed a robust, online/offline capable management system featuring dynamic dashboards that track funding sources and assistance distribution in real-time.

The Impact

Prevented duplicate claims and unauthorized assistance via automated eligibility checks, while providing leadership with instant visibility into fund utilization.

04

Accounting Information System

The Challenge

Finance teams were burdened by "fragmented data," manually navigating and cross-referencing multiple MS Excel spreadsheets to verify accounting entries.

The IANDEV Solution

Consolidated legacy spreadsheets into a centralized, relational database system with automated entry validation and cross-tabulation features.

The Impact

Reduced data entry time and virtually eliminated human error, allowing the accounting team to focus on financial analysis rather than manual data encoding.

02

Biometric Logs System

The Challenge

An agency’s existing biometric hardware and software produced raw logs that failed to meet the strict reporting and auditing standards of the Commission on Audit (COA).

The IANDEV Solution

We engineered a custom middleware layer that extracts raw biometric data and re-formats it into COA-compliant Daily Time Records (DTR).

The Impact

Achieved 100% audit compliance and eliminated manual data corrections, ensuring seamless payroll processing and regulatory peace of mind.

Accounting Management System

Graphic Design
01

Rel-Event Creations

The Challenge

An events design needed a professional visual identity that would attract clients. The task was to create a branding system that was simple yet creative and artistic, ensuring it remained easily memorable in a competitive industry.

The iandsigns Solution

We developed a concept-driven logo combining the Ananse Ntontan symbol—representing wisdom and creativity—with festive balloon elements. The palette used a vibrant gradient from Dark Red to Selective Yellow to reflect the energy of entertainment and art.

02

Los Nativos Coffee

View Branding Styleguide
The Challenge

A specialty coffee company required a brand identity that felt both ancestral and modern. The goal was to create a logo that honors heritage while standing out in the contemporary "third-wave" coffee market.

The iandsigns Solution

We engineered a symbolic fusion by embedding the Mayan Lamat symbol (representing new beginnings and abundance) into the center of a coffee bean. This was paired with a sophisticated "Russet" and "Very Dark Cyan" color palette to evoke the earthiness of coffee soil and the richness of the brew.

The Impact

Delivered a comprehensive branding styleguide including responsive logo variations (Large, Medium, Small) and precise CMYK/RGB color specifications, ensuring brand consistency across both digital platforms and physical packaging.

03

The Great Provider

View Full Case Study
The Challenge

A general insurance agency required a visual "re-engineering" of their legacy brand. The goal was to move away from a cluttered, traditional aesthetic toward a formal, minimal, and modern identity that represents trustworthiness and stability.

The iandsigns Solution

We developed a symbolic narrative titled "The Flow of Grace." The design integrates a Triangle (representing the Holy Trinity), a Circle (the human race/clients), and a Hand (divine protection). By using a "Blue Dianne" and "Tango" palette, we balanced corporate firmness with the energy of service.

The Impact

Transformed the agency's presence with a "digital-friendly" flat design that ensures legibility across all mediums—from large-scale outdoor signage to small-format insurance documents and digital IDs.

DailyAsk Privacy

Transparency, faith, and your data.

Zero Data Collection

We believe your spiritual journey is personal. DailyAsk does not collect, track, or share any personal identifiers. There are no accounts to create and no cookies to track you.

Local-First Storage

All your customizations—including Widget Opacity and Dark Mode settings—are stored exclusively on your device using encrypted local preferences. This data never leaves your phone.

Service Connections

To provide daily reflections, the app securely communicates with:

  • Inadiutorium API: Fetches the NABRE Gospel via HTTPS.
  • Reflection Engine: Generates AI insights based solely on the scripture text.
